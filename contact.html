Collaboration

This wiki will focus on documenting the collaborative process. An important aspect of this section will be the group charter that you will draft together.

This section can include any number of notes, meeting minutes, group brainstorming sessions, group drawings, etc. It should also include a clear outline of the division of labor for each staging post in the project. The section should be a reflection of the collaboration you are engaging and the iterative process of creating the various aspects of the project in a collaborative setting.
